For Visual Studios.

1) #include "library.h"
2) Configuration Properties/VC++ Directories/Include Directories
	(click and edit, add new entry)
3) Project/properties/Configuration Properties/VC++ Directories/Library Directories
	(click and edit, add new entry)
4) Configuration Properties/Linker/Input/Additional Dependencies (e.g.: library.lib)
5) Place .dll files either in the directory you'll be opening your final .exe or
   into Windows/system32
